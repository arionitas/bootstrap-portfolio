About Me

As the co-founder of Konnect Studios, I embarked on an exhilarating journey into the realm of music production, immersing myself in the intricacies of business, logistics, and the world of start-ups. This venture bestowed upon me not just invaluable insights but also a diverse skill-set sculpted through overcoming challenges and crafting the creation of a company from the ground up. My journey has primed me with a tapestry of abilities, which I'm now poised to weave into the fabric of my exciting new path. Eager to infuse innovation and vitality into my next endeavour, I'm thrilled to explore the possibilities ahead.

2020 Abduction - Sultvn

Sultvn, A producer and engineer hailing from Kazakhztan, Atyrau, had a project on the go called '2020' and pictured a theme revoling around space for his EP Release. I collaged a series of images together to create a world that depicts an Alien Abduction of his car. Along with this I added various elements like his headphones, Ableton Push device and other items floating above the car to give the sensation that this producers sounds are out of this world.
